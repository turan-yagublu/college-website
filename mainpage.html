XYZ University's Mission Statement

XYZ University is a university that is, first and foremost, committed to giving its students the best possible education. Our mission is to be indiscriminant when doing so, regardless of race, religion, nationality, age, class, or technological illiteracy. This is why we have made it a point to make our website simplified, so that we don't create an unnecessary barrier for our current, as well as potential students.
